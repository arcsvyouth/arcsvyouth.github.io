Silicon Valley Chapter Youth

Welcome to the chapter!

The Silicon Valley Red Cross chapter oversees Red Cross activity for the region, including ~30 high school clubs. As a member of a club, you are also a member of the chapter and have access to many excellent volunteering opportunities.

Chapter opportunities

We connect youth with chapter-wide events like Sound the Alarm, disaster relief, and more. Youth have the opportunity to work with Red Cross staff and adult volunteers.

Committees

The chapter youth leadership hold yearlong volunteer groups with a variety of topics for all youth to join. Applications closed September 30th and decisions have been released.

Support for clubs

All our events are open to club members and count for volunteer hours. In addition, we assist in blood drives and hold outreach events at schools, like Community Disaster Education.

Battle of the Bays

What is Battle of the Bays?

Battle of the Bays is a competition between the East Bay, West Bay, and Silicon Valley Red Cross chapters to have the most volunteer hours logged in Volunteer Connection in the month of March.

What are the prizes?

  • $500 in a fundraiser: Pizza party for your club.
  • Show 10 hours logged in March: Get a pin.
  • Show 20 hours logged in March: Get a pin, a free Red Cross t-shirt, and get featured on our social media.
  • Club logs 100+ hours in March: Get a certificate from Red Cross, get featured as club of the month, get a donut party.

Chat the ARCSV Youth facebook page with a screenshot and the email you use for Volunteer Connection to get your prize!

How can you help?

  1. Log your hours into Volunteer Connection right after you volunteer.
  2. Volunteer more with the Red Cross! Attend committee meetings, participate in committee events, explore some other volunteer opportunities in the chapter.
  3. Keep up to date with our upcoming events. All chapter events count toward Battle of the Bays.

How can your club help?

  1. Keep members informed about upcoming chapter and Red Cross events.
  2. Make sure members log club meeting hours into Volunteer Connection.

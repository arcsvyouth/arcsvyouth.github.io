Upcoming Events

Join the Silicon Valley Chapter Liaison Google Group!

In order to communicate with all Red Cross youth in the Silicon Valley, we would like you to join the chapter's Google Group. Officers at December's liaison meeting were informed of this and should be adding their club members to the list.

Amateur Radio Training

On January 27, from 3:30 to 5:30PM, the Disaster Committee will be hosting a radio training at the Silicon Valley Chapter. The head of radio communications at the Chapter will teach amateur radio skills that can be used in the case of a disaster cutting out communication lines. It's a very hands-on training (there will be radios to play with) so don't miss out!

Fisher House Dinner

The Service to the Armed Forces committee is hosting another Fisher House dinner on February 16th at 4:30. There will be a Valentine's Day themed menu of pasta dishes, a taco bar, cupcakes, brownies, rice, and vegetables. The dinner will be for around 25-30 people, and no seafood is allowed. If anyone is available to attend the dinner or would like to provide food, please contact the SAF coordinator Abha at redcrossarmedforces@gmail.com.

Fisher House Christmas Packages

SAF is collecting donations to send to Fisher House. Fisher House is an organization that houses veterans’ families as the vets undergo treatment at the VA Hospital in Palo Alto. In order to make them feel at home for the holidays, we are creating small packages for the children and families, and we would like all clubs and volunteers to participate. Sign up to bring donations at tiny.cc/fisherhouse. You will be accountable for buying and bringing in the item by December 16th. Contact the SAF coordinators for any questions.

Raid Cross is on 1/13 (Saturday) 1-4PM

Raid Cross is a role-playing simulation activity devised by the French and Belgian Red Cross societies to help young people aged 14-21 explore:

  • The humanitarian issues involved in armed conflict situations.
  • The basic rules of International Humanitarian Law (IHL) that apply in those situations.
  • The importance of these rules in conflict situations, including preventing civilian casualties and excessive damage to civilian objects, e.g., schools, homes, cities and towns.

This event is an excellent opportunity for youth to participate in Red Cross and learn more about international involvement, and we encourage everyone to participate if they can! Email svrc.internationals@gmail.com with any questions!

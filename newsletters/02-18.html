It is mandatory for each club to sign up for one CDE. Americorps and Disaster Committee members will come to your school to give members a 20-30 minute disaster preparedness presentation. Check the "Contact" page to email the Americorps and schedule one.

Fisher House Dinner

The Service to the Armed Forces committee is hosting another Fisher House dinner on February 16th at 4:30. There will be a Valentine's Day themed menu of pasta dishes, a taco bar, cupcakes, brownies, rice, and vegetables. The dinner will be for around 25-30 people, and no seafood is allowed. If anyone is available to attend the dinner or would like to provide food, please contact the SAF coordinator Abha at redcrossarmedforces@gmail.com.

Ice Skating Social

In collaboration, the Red Cross fundraising coordinators and Spirit Committee are hosting an Ice Skating Social Fundraiser on Saturday, February 24th from 1:15 to 3:15pm at Solar4America Ice in San Jose! This is a great opportunity to have a great time with friends WHILE helping the American Red Cross. It’s $17 per person including admissions and skate rentals. ALL proceeds go towards furthering the mission of the American Red Cross. RSVP at: https://tinyurl.com/skatesocial.

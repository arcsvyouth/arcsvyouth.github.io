Ongoing Event Dates and Time Description
Eboard Coordinator Apps due April 28 Applications for the 2017 - 2018 year of Silicon Valley Youth Executive Board are now open!
E-Board is the highest possible leadership volunteer opportunity under the local Red Cross. We serve to implement projects and programs for our youth.
You will find more information about E-Board and specific positions available at https://drive.google.com/drive/u/0/folders/0B6HC9WbxAhj-MWppS2x3ZGw0MU0. It is also required to provide a recommendation with the following format, however current E-Board members do not need one.
Applications for Coordinator Positions are due April 28th at 11:59 pm. Late applications will not be accepted.
If you have any further questions, please email at arcsv.youthliaison@gmail.com or chat the ARCSV page.
We encourage all interested to apply!
Operation Care and Comfort ends April 30 Operation Care and Comfort provides support and comfort to US military serivce members through care packages. Drop off donations at the San Jose Red Cross Chapter. The care package item list is here.
Location: Red Cross Chapter
Youth Volunteer Program at Palo Alto VA app begins 3/1 Palo Alto Veterans' Affairs Hospital has a summer opportunity for students age 14 or over to volunteer a minimum of 100hours between 6/12-8/28/17. The applications are open from 3/1 until all positions have been filled. The application is at http://www.paloalto.va.gov/docs/volunteer_application_packet.pdf. For more information, visit http://www.paloalto.va.gov/giving/.

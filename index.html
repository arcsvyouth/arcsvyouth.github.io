Ongoing Event Dates and Time Description
Battle of the Bays March Battle of the Bays is a competition taking place in March between the Silicon Valley and the East and West Bay Chapters for the most hours per volunteer. For every few hours you earn, you will receive a Silicon Valley badge on your profile picture by visiting Battle of the Bays Profile Badge Generator!
Leadership Development Conference Application due March 31 The Leadership Development Center (LDC) is a four-day overnight conference designed by youth for youth that provides learning experiences focused on leadership development, community involvement, and disaster preparedness. Delegates will become trained community disaster educators and learn how to start and effectively run a Red Cross Club. Additionally, delegates will gain invaluable experience in activities such as team-building, public speaking, diversity training, CPR/First Aid, disaster and emergency preparedness, and international humanitarian law. No previous Red Cross experience is required to apply. The application is at tinyurl.com/rc17ldc.
Operation Care and Comfort ends April 30 Operation Care and Comfort provides support and comfort to US military serivce members through care packages. Drop off donations at the San Jose Red Cross Chapter. The care package item list is here.
Location: Red Cross Chapter
Youth Volunteer Program at Palo Alto VA app begins 3/1 Palo Alto Veterans' Affairs Hospital has a summer opportunity for students age 14 or over to volunteer a minimum of 100hours between 6/12-8/28/17. The applications are open from 3/1 until all positions have been filled. The application is at http://www.paloalto.va.gov/docs/volunteer_application_packet.pdf. For more information, visit http://www.paloalto.va.gov/giving/.

Park Cleanup March 18, 8:45AM-1PM We recently became aware of a volunteering event in San Jose to help clean up areas that have been affected by the recent flooding. The flood devastated a lot of parkland, so we need many helping hands to clear flood debris and remove the muck that the water left behind!
Please wear closed-toe shoes, long pants and long sleeves. You'll be working near the Japanese Friendship Garden, as well as Happy Hollow Park and Zoo. There is a lot of cleaning to be done, in order to restore and reopen some of our most popular city parks!
For more information and to pre-register, please enter your name and email address here: http://tinyurl.com/floodsignup.
Come and help for the entire time, or drop-in for just an hour or two. We need your assistance!
If you have any further questions, feel free to contact Mollie Tobias at 408 595-3483 or email SJVolunteer@sanjoseca.gov
To sign up, you must first create a profile, but it's very quick!
Location: Kelley Park Complex, 1300 Senter Road, San Jose, 95112

Battle of the Bays

What is Battle of the Bays?

Battle of the Bays is a competition between the East Bay, West Bay, and Silicon Valley Red Cross chapters to have the most volunteer hours logged in Volunteer Connection.

What are the prizes?

$500 in a fundraiser: Pizza party for your club.

Leadership Development Center 2018

As summer approaches, the Red Cross is proud to announce that applications for our 2018 Leadership Development Center are open for submission!

The Leadership Development Center will be held from June 18-21 at the Redwood Glen in Loma Mar, California. Designed to cultivate core leadership skills such as teamwork, public speaking, and diversity awareness, the Leadership Development Center builds confidence and long-lasting friendships. Through service learning and hands-on, interactive games, participants leave empowered to serve their communities.

If you are entering grades 9-12 and interested in four days of CPR/First Aid training, leadership training, disaster preparedness workshops, and meeting Red Cross youth from throughout Northern California, sign up at this link today! The deadline for applications is April 15th, 2018.

The cost to attend camp is $250. We will send out more information on the details of camp, payment, and more in late April once acceptances have been sent. Scholarships are available to attend camp.

To learn more, please visit our website: http://www.redcross.org/local/california/northern-california-coastal/volunteer/youth-volunteer-services/leadership-development-center

Questions can be directed to Allie Parker at alexandra.parker@redcross.org or Tori Gottberg at victoria.gottberg@redcross.org. We can't wait to see you this summer!

Veterans' Day Posters

Since Veterans Day is also coming up, the Service to the Armed Forces coordinators are planning to make posters for the parade. The posters are a replacement for the holiday cards for veterans event, and we encourage everyone to make a poster if they have some time. Make sure that it will be easy to carry in the parade, and turn it in to either Abha or Jiho before November 10th. You can either drop it off at the youth services room at the chapter or to Abha directly, but please let her know beforehand by emailing her at redcrossarmedforces@gmail.com. Please also direct any questions you have to that email.

Silicon Valley Red Cross Merchandise Fundraiser

Hey guys! We're excited to announce that our annual Silicon Valley Red Cross Merchandise Fundraiser has begun! This year, we have a variety of apparel, accessories, other merchandise such as water bottles available.
All profits go towards supporting the American Red Cross. Please spread the news to your committee members!
This google form includes details about the merchandise that can be purchased for this fundraiser. All orders must be placed through this specific Google form: http://tinyurl.com/rcmerch17
The form will close Saturday, November 25th by 11:59pm. Payments will be collected by Sunday, December 3rd at the liaison meeting, unless other arrangements are made with us.

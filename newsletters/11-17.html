November Newsletter

Spotlight

This month's spotlight is on National Youth Involvement Month!

NYIM is a national American Red Cross initiative that aims to bolster creativity and participation from Red Cross youth volunteers. It is a month dedicated to celebrating youth involvement, especially by shining a spotlight on excellent youth activities and by hosting youth events. The National Youth Council leads the creative process behind NYIM each year, and this year, they created the NYIM theme of Travel Beyond the Boundaries (#TBTB). Since we think all Red Cross youth volunteers go beyond the boundaries to create change, we encourage clubs to incorporate this theme into events during NYIM.

1. Take pictures with our frame to support #TBTB!

2. Overlay your profile picture with #TBTB!

3. Look out for a merch giveaway soon! :)

Veterans' Day

Parade

Sign up for the parade at tiny.cc/vetsparade by 11/9 midnight. The parade is on Nov. 11 10AM-2PM. All youth are invited!

Posters

Since Veterans Day is also coming up, the Service to the Armed Forces coordinators are planning to make posters for the parade. The posters are a replacement for the holiday cards for veterans event, and we encourage everyone to make a poster if they have some time. Make sure that it will be easy to carry in the parade, and turn it in to either Abha or Jiho before November 10th. You can either drop it off at the youth services room at the chapter or to Abha directly, but please let her know beforehand by emailing her at redcrossarmedforces@gmail.com. Please also direct any questions you have to that email.

December Recap

Community Disaster Education (CDE)

It is mandatory for each club to sign up for one CDE. Americorps and Disaster Committee members will come to your school to give members a 20-30 minute disaster preparedness presentation. Check the "Contact" page to email the Americorps and schedule one.

Fisher House Christmas Packages

Over the holidays, SAF committee collected donations from all youth to give to veteran families at Fisher House!

Winter Social

Spirit Committee had a lovely end-of-the-semester social with hot chocolate, snacks, and cookie decorating.

Volunteer Connection

Confused about setting up a Volunteer Connection account? Try this guide.

Join the Silicon Valley Chapter Liaison Google Group!

In order to communicate with all Red Cross youth in the Silicon Valley, we would like you to join the chapter's Google Group. Officers at December's liaison meeting were informed of this and should be adding their club members to the list.

Amateur Radio Training

Disaster Committee is inviting the radio communications of Silicon Valley Chapter to train youth on radio operation! Radio is essential for communication if phone lines are down during a disaster. Come to the chapter at January 27 3:30-5:30PM to get trained!

Spotlight

This month's spotlight is on Youth Executive Board Applications!

Are you interested in joining the leadership team behind committees and chapter events? Silicon Valley's Eboard (Youth Executive Board) applications are open!

First, read about our different open positions and its responsibilities.

The two parts of the application are:

  • Applicant Information and Resume
  • Recommendation form

If you are applying for co-chair, please submit both parts of the application by March 30 to eboardapps@gmail.com.

If you are applying for coordinator, please submit both parts of the application by April 20 to eboardapps@gmail.com.

Sound the Alarm

Sound the Alarm for Silicon Valley is almost here! Help us #EndHomeFires by installing smoke alarms and providing fire safety education within your community. Our Sound the Alarm installation events are held every Saturday from April 28th to May 12th. Sign up on the Volunteer Connection calendar!

The closest locations to the Silicon Valley Chapter are:
Saturday, April 28 - Milpitas
Saturday, May 5 - San Jose (Berryessa)
Saturday, May 12 - San Jose (Edenvale)

Learn more: https://arcsvyouth.github.io/info/2018-metrics.html#soundthealarm.

Volunteer Connection Youth Application work-around

Hello all,

Please be advised that the usual Youth Application on Volunteer Connection is currently unavailable due to system changes. If you have new members who are trying to make their Volunteer Connection accounts, please direct them through these steps:

  1. Go to http://www.redcross.org/volunteer/become-a-volunteer
  2. Click "Apply Now" under All Other Volunteer Applications
  3. Complete the 4 General Application steps and accept the Terms&Conditions

You do not have to input your Social Security Number or complete the background check. Once all new members have completed the steps above, club officers should email me a member roster (full name, phone, email) so that I can convert those accounts to their proper Youth Status. Without this email, I cannot approve accounts, so please be sure to complete this final step.

An update will be sent out when the Youth Application is once again accessible. Thank you for your patience, and please direct any questions or concerns to me.

Sound the Alarm

Sound the Alarm for Silicon Valley is here! Help us #EndHomeFires by installing smoke alarms and providing fire safety education within your community. Our Sound the Alarm installation events are held every Saturday from April 28th to May 12th. Sign up on the Volunteer Connection calendar!

The closest locations to the Silicon Valley Chapter are:
Saturday, April 28 - Milpitas
Saturday, May 5 - San Jose (Berryessa)
Saturday, May 12 - San Jose (Edenvale)

Learn more: https://arcsvyouth.github.io/info/2018-metrics.html#soundthealarm.

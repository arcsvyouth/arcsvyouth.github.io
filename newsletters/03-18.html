March Newsletter

Spotlight

This month's spotlight is on CPR/First Aid Training. The Silicon Valley chapter is offering two CPR/First Aid trainings in March and two more in April. This training is free if you have at least 10 approved Red Cross hours in Volunteer Connection. Learn more and sign up.

February Recap

Fisher House Dinner

SAF committee made a Valentines Day themed dinner for Fisher House residents. Check out a recap video on our facebook page by Abha Patkar!

Ice Skating Social

Spirit and Fundraising held an exciting Ice Skating Social at Solar4America Ice in San Jose. Thank you to our youth who came out to have fun while supporting the Red Cross!

Pillowcase Training Day

This two-hour in person training was another step to certify youth volunteers as Pillowcase Project instructors. Now, these volunteers can shadow certified instructors to teach children disaster preparedness, safety, and coping methods.

Disaster Simulation

The Disaster Committee is hosting a simulation of home fires, earthquakes, floods, and more. Learn basic first aid and how to effectively respond to disasters through these interactive presentations! The event is at the chapter on March 10, 2-5pm and all youth are welcome to participate.

For more information, contact the Disaster Coordinators at rcdisastercommittee@gmail.com.
